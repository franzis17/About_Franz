Student Credit Assessment System

What is the app about?

The app enables institutions to efficiently assess a student's CRL (Credit Recognised Learning). CRL is a process where a student's previous learning or qualifications is evaluated to determine if they can count towards a new course or degree. The main users are the institution's assessors or unit coordinators that evaluate students previous academic credits.

Why this system is needed?

Our client institution's traditional methods for assessing a new student's CRL involves exchanging Excel files containing hundreds of student evaluations. This process is inefficient, as assessors communicate with each other via email, adding unnecessary overhead time. To streamline this process, our team implemented a centralised database that stores all students' CRL information, allowing multiple assessors to access it easily.
